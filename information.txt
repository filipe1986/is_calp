Direct Market Access (DMA) via Colocation using the [B3 Binary UMDF](https://www.b3.com.br/en_us/solutions/platforms/puma-trading-system/for-developers-and-vendors/binary-umdf/) is the highest tier of connectivity for the Brazilian exchange. It is designed for low-latency, high-performance trading by placing your hardware inside the [B3 Data Center](https://www.b3.com.br/en_us/solutions/hosting-co-location/data-center/data-center/) alongside the PUMA Trading System. [1, 2, 3] 
## Technical Details of B3 Binary UMDF

* Protocol: Uses Simple Binary Encoding (SBE), a fixed-position protocol that allows direct data access without the need for sequential field processing.
* Transport: Delivered via UDP Multicast, providing the fastest possible dissemination for Equities, Derivatives (including Mini Dólar), and FX market segments.
* Order Book Architecture: Supports Market By Order (MBO), which provides full depth of the order book, allowing you to see every individual order rather than just aggregated price levels.
* Latency: Offers significantly lower latency than the legacy FIX/FAST format by reducing the time required for encoding and decoding. [4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10] 

## Colocation Environment

* Facility: Located in a Tier-3 certified data center in São Paulo, providing redundant power and cooling systems.
* Hardware Hosting: B3 provides physical space and racks where customers can install their own servers.
* Cross-Connects: Low-latency physical cables connect your equipment directly to the exchange’s matching engines.
* Support: Managed by technical teams available 24/7 for incident resolution and equipment management. [1, 3, 9, 11, 12] 

## Onboarding and Costs

* Contracting: Requires a relationship with a clearing member (broker) who "sponsors" your access.
* Certification: All software must be certified by the B3 Certification and Testing Center before it can connect to the live production environment.
* Estimated Fees:
* Monthly Colocation Fees: Can range significantly depending on rack space and power requirements.
   * Connectivity Fees: Monthly costs for market data and order entry cross-connects are typically several hundred dollars each (e.g., approximately $575 for certain high-speed cross-connects).
   * Activation Fees: Initial setup fees are also required for most cross-connect services. [2, 13, 14, 15]
